Around Me uses your phone's internal GPS or your Web browsers geolocations settings (Google Chrome, Firefox and IE 9+) to find services, post a micro ads to buy and sell your stuff.

We use the same principle of traditional classified ads of newspapers and took them to new technologies, focusing on mobile devices (smartphones or tablets), but improving the user experience and simplifying searches for websites that offer additional services such as Craigslist, eBay, OLX etc.

Around Me search engine and publication is global, your ad would be available within a radius of 20km or 20 miles, however, you can modify the search global ads to show.

Around Me search engine focuses on the location of the user to display the results of the searches and and you can use tags labels for improvement the results. No matter if you are in Paris or Buenos Aires, you can find your stuff in a simple and fast way to do it..

FAQ

What does "Around Me" works?
Around Me uses your phone's internal GPS or your Web browsers geolocations settings (Google Chrome, Firefox and IE 9+) to find services, post a micro ads to buy and sell your stuff. We use the same principle of traditional classified ads of newspapers and took them to new technologies, focusing on mobile devices (smartphones or tablets), but improving the user experience and simplifying searches for websites that offer additional services such as Craigslist, eBay, OLX etc.
What makes us different from Craiglist or others?
Our search engine focuses on the location of the user to display the results of the searches and category labels for improvement, ie If you are in New York City or San Francisco Bay, you can find a new office just make a search: Pizza in New York or office in Mountain View for startup. This will allow you to filter your results by: mountain view, office, start up.
My privacy is important, I'm exposed to some type of fraud or other crime?
We have thought of almost everything, and we have implemented a verified user system to meet our users and we primarily use the data entered in the Facebook and Twitter profiles to validate credentials and users, so you'll know who you're doing business.
Any costs of publication of ads?
Yes of course, there are costs of maintaining our platform (servers, domains, programmers, etc.), however, publication costs are so low that everybody will be able to publish your ads for unlimited time and pay from any device through cards credit, paypal or others.
I am a backer of the project, Should pay for my ads?
Not necessarily, since you are an important part of this project all you want to publish ads will be free for unlimited time, also, to have all added values ​​such as "verified user", photos, featured listings as well as any other value to be added in the future to "Around Me".
There are limitations to the results of "Around Me"?
Our search engine and publication is global, your ad would be available within a radius of 20km or 20 miles, however, you can modify the search global ads to show.
